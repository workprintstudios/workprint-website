Rankball

By Workprint Studios Dev Team

Track. Evaluate. Empower.

Rankball is designed for coaches who want to understand their players like never before. Whether you're observing skills during evaluations, monitoring growth over a season, or keeping long-term records, Rankball offers a simple and intuitive way to rank player abilities.

Currently tailored for baseball, Rankball is evolving to support football, soccer, and many more sports, giving coaches tools to elevate their teams across multiple disciplines.

Why Rankball?

We’ve built Rankball to be ambitious yet grounded: an assistant for coaches that reduces guesswork, keeps players’ progress transparent, and allows thoughtful decisions based on real data. Imagine a tool that grows with your team, helping you discover patterns, strengths, and areas for improvement—all in one place.

Whether you’re running tryouts, tracking season-long performance, or developing your star athletes for the future, Rankball gives you clarity and perspective without overcomplicating the process.

What's Next

Our vision is to expand Rankball into a versatile platform for all sports, keeping the experience seamless and intuitive. Coaches will be able to track, compare, and understand their players across multiple sports while keeping data organized and actionable.
