1. Information We Collect

Firebase Authentication

When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and display name to identify your account and personalize your experience.

Firestore / Realtime Database

We store any data you generate in the app (e.g., saved game data or CSV files) in Firebase to enable your gameplay across devices.

Ads (Google AdMob)

If you use the free version, ads are shown. Google may collect device information to personalize ads. No personally identifiable information is shared with us through AdMob.

Subscription / Payment Info

Payments are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information. Your subscription status is verified through Google Play.

  • To provide subscription-based features and remove ads for paid users.

3. Data Sharing

4. Your Rights
